# Privacy notice

## Purpose limitation

Tenant details are used only to process and coordinate property maintenance.

## Data minimisation

Only information necessary for maintenance is requested (such as unit, name, mobile number, issue details, and supporting photos).

## Storage and systems

This website does not store tenant submissions. Submission data is captured in Google Forms/Drive used for maintenance operations.

## Verification

Requests may be verified against managing agent tenant records to confirm eligibility and reduce misuse.

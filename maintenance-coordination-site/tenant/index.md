# Tenant maintenance entry

<a class="button button-large" href="{{ site.tenant_intake_form_url }}" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Log a maintenance issue</a>

> Requests are verified against the managing agent’s tenant register (unit + mobile). Unverified requests will not be actioned.

## Maintenance emergency vs personal emergency

**Maintenance emergency** (log via the form, then follow building protocol):
- Active leak causing ongoing property damage
- Electrical hazard (e.g. exposed live wires, burning smell)
- Sewer overflow affecting safe use
- Loss of essential service creating immediate property/safety risk

**Personal or life-threatening emergency**:
- Crime in progress, personal danger, violence, or medical crisis
- Call SAPS **10111** or **112 (mobile)** immediately
- GBV support: **0800 428 428**, “Please call me” **\*120\*7867#**, SMS **help** to **31531**

Tenants must use the form for maintenance. No tenant WhatsApp support is provided.
